Thomas T Pham

Systems Administrator / Engineer

I’m a Systems Administrator with over 20 years of experience in IT. I have advanced working knowledge and extensive experience with Wintel Systems including Active Directory and have obtained an MCP in Microsoft Exchange while working towards MCSE Certification.

I have excellent Backups administration skills and proven ability with VMware, Cisco Networking (training for CCNA), Security systems (Firewall & Anti-Virus) as well as other technologies such as SAN/NAS (EMC & Pure Storage) and Cloud (AWS & Azure).

My verbal and written communication is excellent and I also have great planning and organisational skills supported by Project Management experience and ITIL credentials.

I possess advanced analytical troubleshooting techniques which enables me to have strong problem-solving capabilities.

I also have a background in Customer Services to compliment my solid Technical skills having started my ICT career as a Help Desk Analyst after graduating with an Associate Diploma in Business Computing.

Having been employed by a top National Law Firm for 10 years and recently by a household Energy company, I’m well equipped to work under high pressure and have experience supporting large scale Infrastructure environments and over 1,200 staff across various locations.

You’ll discover that I’m a very quick learner and always a team player so I would not have any difficulty fitting in with any group or environment.
